How would you describe moroccan food?

Moroccan cuisine is very healthy, in general. Most dishes contain vegetables and rely on whole grains, freshly prepared food, spices and sweet fruit rather than refined sugar and deep frying. we use lamb which is leaner than red meat, and couscous which is healthier than rice. the delicious combination of mouth-watering flavors is what makes it unique.
